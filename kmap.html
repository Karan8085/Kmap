Karnaugh Map (K-Map) Solver

A tool to simplify boolean expressions up to 4 variables.

A Karnaugh map (K-map) is a graphical method used to simplify Boolean algebra expressions. It reduces the need for extensive calculations by allowing users to identify and eliminate redundant terms through visual grouping.
